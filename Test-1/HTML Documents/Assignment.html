How to Make Limenade

Limenade

Ingredients

3 limes, sugar, water

Directions

Halve the limes, then squeeze the juice from the halves using the juicer or reamer.

Measure the juice.

Mix one part juice to three parts water.

Stir in two parts sugar.

Add ice when serving.
